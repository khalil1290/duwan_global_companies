Our Story

OUR COMPANY DUWAN COMPANY LIMITED is a Limited Liability Company, incorporated on the 27 day of January, 1997 with RC No: 306855, under the Companies Act of 1990 by the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The registered office is No.12A Nnamdi Azikiwe Way, Unguwa Ma’azu, Kaduna, with branches in Kano and Jos.

Since incorporation of the company, it has continued to grow and presently is one of the major dealers and importer of explosives materials in Nigeria. The company is a dynamic enterprise that is engaged in civil explosives business, mining exploration and quarry activities under the solid mineral sector, after realizing that Nigeria is endowed with abundant mineral resources. The company has licence to manufacture explosives, permit to import explosives, licence to sell explosives and other regulatory related documents.

We are a precision drilling and blasting company specializing in the application of explosives for controlled blasting for the excavation of rock to the exact specification and expectations. DUWAN DRILLING & BLASTING is right around the corner at your service with the kind of quarry blasting service you’ve not yet experienced. Our Drilling & Blasting Division has serviced many big quarries across the Northern region.

Our Blasting expert utilizes the latest technologies to give our client a high yield and provide a 100% crushable service.
